Lin Groundwater Intelligence Group

We develop analytical, numerical, and data-assisted methods for groundwater decisions.

The group is directed by Ying-Fan Lin and focuses on aquifer-test interpretation, Lagging Theory, transformation uncertainty, and groundwater-informed subsurface energy.

We study how measured drawdown, recovery, hydraulic gradients, and thermal response become interpreted parameters and design limits. The common question is when the model used for interpretation changes the decision.
