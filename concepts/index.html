Concept system

One asynchronous-response idea, four research layers.

The research program begins with a simple observation: field response may not move in step with the forcing or gradient assumed by a classical model. The framework then asks which mechanism creates the asynchrony, how it should be modeled, and whether the interpretation changes decisions.
