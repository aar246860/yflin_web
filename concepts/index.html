Research concepts

From delayed response to groundwater decisions.

The research program begins with a simple observation: field drawdown, recovery, head, or temperature may not move in step with the forcing or gradient assumed by a classical model. The next question is which mechanism creates the lag, how it should be modeled, and whether the interpretation changes a decision.
