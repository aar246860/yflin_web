Asynchrony Effects in Thermal Response Tests

TRT and subsurface heat projects inherit groundwater uncertainty when flow, boundary, storage, or memory effects are active.

The practical question is not whether a model can fit a response. It is whether the interpretation pathway changes the decision variable: pumping limit, recovery time, thermal margin, or uncertainty buffer.

Scientific caution: this is a screening and interpretation issue, not a universal claim that every site requires a memory-aware model.
