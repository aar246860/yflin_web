# Source notes

- Lin, Chang, and Tsai (2022), *Journal of Hydrology*, DOI `10.1016/j.jhydrol.2022.127920`.
- Existing analytical inversions commonly assumed time-invariant vertical groundwater flux. The paper derives an analytical heat-transfer model and inverse approach for transient flux from temperature-depth profiles.
- The analytical model was checked against a finite-element model; synthetic inversion recovered the prescribed transient flux, and the field estimate was close to measured flux.
- Curves in the animation illustrate the paper's measured-object-to-inferred-time-series logic; they are not digitized field observations.
