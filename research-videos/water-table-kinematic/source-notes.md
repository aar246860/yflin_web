# Source notes

- Lin and Lin (2025), *Water Resources Research*, DOI `10.1029/2024WR038724`.
- The paper derives a water-table condition from coupled saturated-unsaturated flow. The reduced condition contains a gravity-drainage time term and a radial-Laplacian term.
- The condition reduces to the classical kinematic form when the radial-Laplacian contribution is negligible.
- Boise Hydrogeophysical Research Site pumping tests support more reliable specific-yield estimates without an additional empirical fitting parameter.
- The animation shows the derivation logic and evidence boundary, not a reproduction of every Boise observation.
