# Source notes

- Lin et al. (2025), *Advances in Water Resources*, DOI `10.1016/j.advwatres.2025.104977`.
- The paper combines an analytical freshwater-lens solution with image wells to represent pumping, recharge, and complex coastline geometry.
- It returns hydraulic head and freshwater-saltwater interface depth, validates the formulation by convergence and comparison tests, and demonstrates pumping optimization for an island example based loosely on Kinmen.
- The animation is conceptual. It does not reproduce an island-specific design calculation or claim that the circular construction alone represents every coastline.
