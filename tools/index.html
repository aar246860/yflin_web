Claim discipline

How to read this page without overclaiming.

The labels are deliberately conservative because groundwater decisions require model-specific evidence, not software branding.

Teaching demo

Good for explanation, not design.

Browser demos show mechanisms and interpretation sensitivity. They do not replace a calibrated aquifer-test analysis or site-specific uncertainty propagation.

Research package

Good for inspection and reproduction.

Research packages are intended to expose code, examples, tests, figures, or manuscript evidence. Their assumptions still control where results can transfer.

Collaboration workflow

Good for confidential or decision-bound work.

When project data are sensitive or decisions are high-stakes, the correct output is a scoped audit or pilot analysis rather than a public demo.

Next step

Bring a dataset, a decision variable, and the analytical model currently being used.

A useful collaboration starts when an existing pumping test, recovery curve, TRT record, or model output has to support a real decision. The tools above are the public inspection layer; the technical work starts with the assumptions behind the data.
